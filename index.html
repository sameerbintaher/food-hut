Green Vegetable

Green leafy vegetables (GLVs) are a blessing for a safe and healthier life and have been in use for centuries. They are considered as an essential part of the diet to meet the daily nutrient requirements. GLVs can be used fresh as a salad or can be cooked/processed as per the interest of the consumer.
